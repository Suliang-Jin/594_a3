📰 Humor Rating Task

Instruction

Welcome to humor rating task! 🎉

In this task, you will see an original news headline and an edited version with one word replaced. Your goal is to rate how funny the edited version is (less funny 👎, mediocre 😐 or funny 👍). Then, please tell us how much you understand the context of the headline. Optionally, you can provide a reasoning on your humor rating.

*Note: the headlines are from real news reports and probably contain political contents. Also, the headlines and the edited versions are directly obtained from a CS publication dataset SemEval-2020 Task 7.

Example

Original headline: WHO calls for elimination of trans fat by 2023

Edited headline: WHO calls for monetization of trans fat by 2023

As you see, the replaced word is elimination → monetization. You can rate the edited headline with [less funny 👎], [mediocre 😐] or [funny 👍].
